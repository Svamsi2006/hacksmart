DEFINITIONS - BATTERY SWAPPING SPECIFIC
Term	Definition
Swap	Complete battery exchange (depleted OUT, charged IN)
Slot	Individual battery storage compartment at station
SOC	State of Charge (0-100%). Acceptable = ≥80%
Low SOC	Battery < 80% charge at pickup
Swap Failure	Customer unable to swap due to station/battery issue
Ghost Swap	System records swap but battery not received
Stuck Battery	Battery locked, unable to remove
Downtime	Period driver unable to earn due to our service failure
Churn Risk	Customer indicating cancellation intent
L1/L2/L3	Agent / Supervisor / Manager escalation levels
 
SOP 1: SWAP STATION FAILURE RESOLUTION
SOP ID: BS-SOP-001-R2 | Priority: CRITICAL

PURPOSE
Resolve station failures immediately to minimize driver downtime. This is the #1 complaint category affecting customer satisfaction and revenue.
SCOPE
•	No batteries available (inventory out)
•	Low SOC batteries (<80% charge)
•	Slot malfunction (door stuck, won't open)
•	Station offline/unreachable
•	QR code scan failure
•	Battery stuck in slot or vehicle
PROCEDURE - STEP BY STEP
STEP 1: IMMEDIATE DATA COLLECTION (< 30 SECONDS)


Agent MUST ask in this exact order:

•	1. "Which station are you at?" → Verify station ID in system
•	2. "What happened when you tried to swap?" → Identify issue type
•	3. "What time did this happen?" → Check against swap logs
•	4. (If battery involved) "What's the battery number?" → Note serial
•	5. Pull up station dashboard WHILE customer speaks (don't ask them to hold)

DO: Navigate system while listening
DON'T: Put customer on hold to search for information


STEP 2: CLASSIFY & ACT (USE DECISION MATRIX)


Issue	L1 Action	TAT	Escalate If
No batteries available	Check nearest station with inventory → Direct customer	< 2 min	None within 3km
Low SOC (60-79%)	Offer: Accept battery + ₹30 credit OR go to alt station	< 3 min	SOC < 60%
Low SOC (< 60%)	Must escalate to L2 for ₹50 credit approval	< 5 min	Auto-escalate
Slot stuck/won't open	Remote unlock attempt → Manual reset guide	< 5 min	Unlock fails
Station offline	Direct to alt station + log outage	< 2 min	Outage > 30min
QR scan fails	Generate manual swap code	< 2 min	3+ failures

STEP 3: STUCK BATTERY - EXACT CUSTOMER INSTRUCTIONS


If battery stuck in VEHICLE:

•	Instruct: "Turn your vehicle completely OFF - not just stopped, turn the key OFF"
•	Instruct: "Put the kickstand DOWN all the way"
•	Instruct: "Press and HOLD the red battery release button for 5 full seconds"
•	Instruct: "While holding, pull the battery handle UP firmly"
•	If 2 attempts fail: "I'm dispatching our team. DO NOT force it. ETA 20 minutes."

If battery stuck in STATION SLOT:

•	Check system: Slot showing "Occupied" or "Available"?
•	If "Occupied": Attempt remote unlock
•	If unlock succeeds: Guide customer to retry
•	If unlock fails: Dispatch field tech (TAT 30 min) + provide alt station

STEP 4: COMPENSATION (STRICT APPROVAL LIMITS)


Scenario	Compensation	Authority	Notes
No batteries at station	₹20 credit OR 10km bonus	Auto-approved (L1)	None
Low SOC 60-79%	₹30 credit	Auto-approved (L1)	None
Low SOC < 60%	₹50 credit	L2 approval REQUIRED	Get approval first
Stuck battery + delay > 15min	₹100 credit + 1 free swap	L2 approval REQUIRED	Get approval first
Station offline → trip cancelled	Case-by-case	L3 ONLY	Immediate escalate

CRITICAL RULE: Agent CANNOT promise > ₹50 without L2 approval. Violation = written warning.


DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS
Every ticket MUST have (incomplete = rejected):
•	Station ID (verified in system)
•	Issue type (from matrix)
•	Timestamp (when it happened, not when they called)
•	Action taken (what you did)
•	Compensation issued (exact amount)
•	Alternative station provided (if yes, which one)
•	Customer sentiment (Calm/Frustrated/Angry)
KPIs - MEASURED DAILY
Metric	Target	Miss Consequence
Average Resolution Time	< 4 minutes	Daily coaching if missed
First Call Resolution	≥ 85%	Performance review
Incorrect compensation	< 2%	Written warning
Incomplete tickets	< 5%	Ticket returned to agent
 
SOP 2: PAYMENT FAILURE & REFUND PROCESSING
SOP ID: BS-SOP-002-R2

PURPOSE
Resolve payment failures and process refunds with strict financial controls. Unauthorized refunds result in HR escalation.
SCOPE
•	Payment gateway failures (Razorpay/Paytm/PhonePe)
•	Double charging / Ghost transactions
•	Refund requests (service failure, cancellation)
•	Auto-debit failures
PAYMENT FAILURE RESOLUTION
STEP 1: VERIFY PAYMENT STATUS

Required info: Transaction ID, Amount, Payment method, Date/time
Check system: Is it "Success," "Failed," or "Pending"?


STEP 2: CLASSIFY & ACT


Issue	L1 Action	TAT	Escalate
Failed + service blocked	Retry with alternate method	< 5 min	None
Success but service not active	Manual activation + flag for refund audit	< 10 min	None
Double charged	ESCALATE TO L2 IMMEDIATELY	Immediate	All cases
Amount incorrect	ESCALATE TO L2 IMMEDIATELY	Immediate	All cases
Pending > 24 hours	Check gateway → Cancel/retry	< 15 min	Still pending after action

CRITICAL: L1 agents CANNOT process refunds. ALL refund requests → Escalate to L2.


REFUND APPROVAL MATRIX (L2/L3 ONLY)
Refund Reason	Authority	Processing Time	Documentation
Duplicate charge	L2 Supervisor	3-5 business days	Both transaction IDs
Ghost swap (not received)	L2 Supervisor	3-5 business days	Swap logs + station camera
Incorrect billing	L2 Supervisor	3-5 business days	Invoice + receipt
Subscription cancel (< 7 days)	L2 Supervisor	5-7 business days	Cancellation request
Subscription cancel (> 7 days)	L3 Manager	7-10 business days	Usage logs + justification
Service quality complaint	L3 Manager	Case-by-case	Evidence + supervisor review

DO: Tell customer: "Refunds take 5-7 business days to reflect in your account"
DON'T: Promise immediate refund or same-day processing
 
SOP 3: SAFETY & EMERGENCY RESPONSE
SOP ID: BS-SOP-003-R2 | Priority: EMERGENCY

PURPOSE
This SOP SUPERSEDES all others when safety is at risk.

EMERGENCY TYPES
Type	Indicators	Response Time
LEVEL 1: Fire/Smoke	Flames, smoke, burning smell	IMMEDIATE (< 60 sec)
LEVEL 2: Severe Overheating	Too hot to touch, swelling	IMMEDIATE (< 2 min)
LEVEL 3: Injury/Accident	Person injured	IMMEDIATE (< 60 sec)
LEVEL 4: Battery Leaking	Liquid/gel visible	URGENT (< 5 min)
LEVEL 1: FIRE/SMOKE - EXACT SCRIPT
EMERGENCY SCRIPT (SAY EXACTLY):


"This is an emergency. Please move away from the battery IMMEDIATELY - at least 10 meters. Do NOT touch it. Do NOT move it. Do NOT pour water on it. Are you safe now?"

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:

•	1. Press RED emergency alert button in CRM (top-right)
•	2. System auto-dispatches: Fire safety team + Field manager + City ops
•	3. Note EXACT location
•	4. Collect: Battery ID, location details, people nearby, fire spreading?
•	5. STAY ON CALL until help arrives or L3 takes over

DO: Keep customer calm, confirm safety
DON'T: Give fire-fighting advice
DON'T: End call before help arrives
LEVEL 3: INJURY - EXACT SCRIPT
SCRIPT:

"I understand you are injured. Have you called an ambulance? [If no] Please call 108 immediately. [If yes] Our manager will call you within 30 minutes. Your ticket number is [ID]."

CRITICAL: Do NOT discuss liability. Do NOT promise compensation. Escalate to L3 + Legal immediately.
